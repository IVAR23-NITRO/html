short poem:

weeping

by federico garcao lorco

weeping,

i go down the street

grotesque,without solution

with the sadness of cyrano

and quixote



redeeming

infinite impossibilites

with the rythm of clock

string bass

by adrian green

some like to dominate

others caress

a voluptuous rythm

on pliant strings

this pulse drives life

through wanton counter point

the heart and harmony

of things.
