BREAD CHILLI

ingredients

  • bread
  • tomato
  • capsicum
  • oil
  • ginger garlic
  • onion
  • tomato sauce
  • red chilli powder

instructions

  1. roast the bread to fine brown colour.
  2. take a pan and pour some oil.
  3. add onion fry till brown.
  4. add some tomatoes and ginger garlic.
  5. fry it and add 1 spoon of salt. add one spoon of red chilli powder.
  6. saute it and add tomato sauce. saute well.
  7. add bread and mix it well

bread chilli is ready
